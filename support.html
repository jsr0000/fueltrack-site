FAQs

How does FuelTrack use my HealthKit data?

FuelTrack reads your workouts and active energy to calculate daily macro targets. All processing happens on your device — your health data is never sent to external servers.

Can I use FuelTrack without Apple Health?

Yes. If you skip HealthKit access, FuelTrack uses your training plan to calculate macro targets instead. You can connect Apple Health at any time from the Profile tab.

How do I change my dietary preferences?

Go to Profile > Preferences > Diet. Changing your diet will regenerate your meal plan to match.

How do I delete my data?

All data is stored on your device. Deleting the app removes everything. You can also sign out from Profile to clear your account link.

What does the barcode scanner do?

Tap "Log Extra" below your meals to scan a packaged food product. FuelTrack looks up the nutritional data and adds it to your daily macro totals.
