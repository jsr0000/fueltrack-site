FuelTrack Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 April 2026

FuelTrack ("we", "our", "the app") is a meal planning and training app for iOS. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

Data stored on your device only

The following data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party:

  • Profile information — name, age, height, weight, sex, dietary preferences, allergies, cooking preferences, and fitness goals
  • Meal plans and shopping lists — your weekly meal plans, recipe ratings, favourites, pinned meals, and shopping list state
  • Training plans — your generated training schedule and session completion history
  • App preferences — appearance, units, notification settings, and other configuration

Apple HealthKit data

With your explicit permission, FuelTrack reads the following data from Apple HealthKit:

  • Active energy burned
  • Workouts (type, duration, distance)
  • Step count
  • Body weight

This data is used solely to personalise your daily macro targets and meal plans based on your training activity. HealthKit data is processed on your device and is never sent to external servers, shared with third parties, or used for advertising.

Camera and barcode scanning

With your permission, FuelTrack accesses your device camera to scan product barcodes. When you scan a barcode, the barcode number is sent to the Open Food Facts API (a free, open-source food database) to retrieve the product name and nutritional information. No personal data, health data, or device identifiers are included in these requests. No images are stored or transmitted — only the barcode number is sent.

Sign in with Apple

If you choose to sign in with Apple, we receive your Apple user identifier. This is stored securely in your device's Keychain. We do not receive or store your email address unless you choose to share it. You can also use the app without signing in.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect analytics or usage data
  • We do not use tracking or advertising frameworks
  • We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to third parties
  • We do not use cookies or web tracking technologies
  • We do not store any data on remote servers (all data stays on your device)

3. Third-Party Services

  • Open Food Facts — when you scan a barcode, the barcode number is sent to the Open Food Facts API to retrieve nutritional data. No personal or health data is included. Open Food Facts is a non-profit, open-source project. See their terms of use.
  • Anthropic Claude API (premium tier, future) — anonymised macro targets and dietary preferences (not personal health data) may be sent to Anthropic's servers for personalised meal plan generation. This will be clearly disclosed before the feature is enabled.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

All your data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all FuelTrack data at any time by:

  • Signing out in the app (Profile > Sign Out)
  • Deleting the app from your device

Since we do not store data on remote servers, deleting the app permanently removes all your data.

5. Children's Privacy

FuelTrack is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at:

Email: privacy@fueltrack.app

8. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it at all times.
